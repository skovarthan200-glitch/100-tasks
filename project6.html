We’re building the future of product development

Nova started with a simple idea: teams should spend time building, not fighting their tools.

Our Story

Founded in 2023 by engineers tired of stitching together 12 different tools to ship one feature, Nova was built to unify the product workflow.

Today, 10,000+ teams use Nova to collect feedback, run experiments, and ship with confidence. We’re remote-first, profitable, and obsessed with developer experience.

What we believe

Build in public

We share our roadmap, changelog, and metrics. Transparency builds trust.

Developers first

If it’s not a joy to use, we don’t ship it. Great DX = great products.

Speed matters

Fast software, fast support, fast iteration. The market doesn’t wait.
