Frequently Asked Questions

Is ZenFlow free?

ZenFlow offers a free 14-day trial with full access to all features. No credit card required. After the trial, paid plans start at $8 per user per month when billed annually. We also have a free plan for teams of up to 3 people.

Does ZenFlow integrate with Slack and GitHub?

Yes. ZenFlow has native two-way integrations with Slack, GitHub, GitLab, Jira, Figma, Notion, and 50+ other tools. You can create tasks from Slack messages, sync GitHub issues, and embed Figma files directly in ZenFlow.

How is ZenFlow different from Jira or Asana?

Unlike Jira which requires admin setup, ZenFlow works out of the box. Unlike Asana which lacks developer features, ZenFlow has native Git integration and sprint planning. We're the only tool that combines docs, tasks, and chat in one AI-powered workspace.

Can I import data from Trello or Monday.com?

Yes. Our migration tool imports boards, tasks, comments, and attachments from Trello, Asana, Monday.com, ClickUp, and Jira. Most teams migrate in under 10 minutes. We also offer free migration assistance for teams over 20 people.

Is my data secure?

ZenFlow is SOC 2 Type II certified and GDPR compliant. All data is encrypted at rest with AES-256 and in transit with TLS 1.3. We offer SSO/SAML, SCIM provisioning, and audit logs on Enterprise plans. Your data is stored in US or EU regions.
