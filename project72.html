08

Voice & Tone

How we sound. We're confident but not arrogant, technical but approachable. We talk like smart humans helping other smart humans.

We Are
Clear: "Automate your workflow in 5 minutes" not "Leverage synergistic paradigms"

Helpful: "Here's how to fix it" not "Contact support"

Confident: "The fastest way to ship" not "We think this might help"
We Are Not
Jargony: Avoid "utilize", "leverage", "synergy"

Salesy: No "revolutionary", "game-changing", "unprecedented"

Robotic: Don't say "Your request has been processed"

Writing Examples

✓ Do: Be Direct
Good: "Connect Slack in one click"
Better: "One click connects Slack"
Why: Active voice, fewer words, same meaning
✗ Don't: Be Vague
Bad: "Streamline your productivity workflow"
Why: Means nothing. What specifically gets better?</
