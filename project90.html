Design Guidelines

  • Keep key elements inside 80% safe zone for iOS rounded corners
  • Test at 29×29px - smallest iOS size. Must stay recognizable
  • Avoid text in icon. Use simple, bold shapes
  • Ensure 4.5:1 contrast ratio for accessibility
  • Android adaptive: Keep important content in center 66dp circle
  • No transparency for iOS App Store submission
